How to play beach ball relay

1.-Each Ball is Labeled A, B, C, D
2.-As a class, you will shout out the correct answer.
3.-One person will select the answer that the class shouted from the basket.
4.-Everyone in the classroom has to touch the ball at least once.
5.-When the beach ball is placed into the basket, the entire class must face forward and sit silently
6.-When everyone is seated and silent, I'll stop the clock and we will discuss the correct answer.
7.-We have to 30 seconds per question to complete this challenge.
